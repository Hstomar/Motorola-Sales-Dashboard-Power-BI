# Motorola Sales Performance Dashboard

[cite_start]This repository features a comprehensive **Sales Performance Dashboard** for Motorola, designed to track market penetration, consumer behavior, and revenue health across major Indian territories[cite: 1, 9, 29]. By synthesizing transaction data into actionable visuals, this dashboard identifies high-value regions and optimizes inventory strategies.

---

### 📊 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
* [cite_start]**Total Sales Revenue:** 769M [cite: 3, 111]
* [cite_start]**Total Quantity Sold:** 19,150 units [cite: 5, 112]
* [cite_start]**Total Transactions:** 3,835 [cite: 6, 113]
* [cite_start]**Average Order Value:** 40.11K [cite: 54, 55]
* [cite_start]**Average Customer Age:** 38.10 years [cite: 114]

---

### 🔍 Strategic Insights
* [cite_start]**Regional Penetration:** Analyzes sales density across key urban hubs including Delhi, Mumbai, Bangalore, and Kolkata to pinpoint market leaders[cite: 10, 21, 22, 29].
* [cite_start]**Model Performance:** Tracks top-performing models—such as the iPhone SE, Galaxy S21, and OnePlus Nord—to align stock with demand[cite: 36, 39, 44, 47].
* [cite_start]**Sales Seasonality:** Monitors monthly volume fluctuations to identify peak performance periods[cite: 56, 76].
* [cite_start]**Payment Preferences:** Visualizes the payment ecosystem, showing a balanced distribution between UPI (26.36%), Credit Cards (24.72%), and Cash (24.69%)[cite: 77, 85, 86, 91, 93].
* [cite_start]**Competitive Comparison:** Benchmarks performance against major competitors like Apple and Samsung to measure market share[cite: 94, 97, 102].
